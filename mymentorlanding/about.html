About MyMentor

MyMentor is a revolutionary AI-powered study tool built specifically for students at Mentor Centre. We're transforming how students learn by combining cutting-edge artificial intelligence with comprehensive learning resources.

Our Mission

We're not here to do the work for you. We're here to help your brain connect the dots faster. MyMentor empowers students to take control of their learning with intelligent tools, comprehensive resources, and 24/7 AI assistance.

Key Features

Cambridge Notes

Comprehensive, well-organized notes aligned with Cambridge curriculum standards. Perfect for revision and understanding core concepts.

AI Companion

Get instant help with assignments and homework. Our AI companion provides explanations, guidance, and support whenever you need it.

Smart Flashcards

Convert your notes into interactive flashcards instantly. Study smarter with spaced repetition and adaptive learning.

AI Overlay Panel

While reading notes, open our AI panel to ask questions about the specific content. Get contextual help in real-time.

Automatic Summaries

Get AI-generated summaries of all your notes. Perfect for quick reviews and understanding key takeaways at a glance.

Generated Exercises

Custom practice problems and exercises generated based on your class materials. Test your knowledge and identify weak areas.

Alumni Network

Connect with alumni and experienced mentors who share their knowledge, insights, and study strategies from their educational journey.

Built by Students, for Students

MyMentor was created with deep understanding of student challenges. We've designed every feature to save you time, reduce stress, and help you achieve better grades while truly understanding the material.
